THE CHALLENGE

Standard text-to-image models often struggle with complex lighting scenarios (like Rembrandt lighting or silhouettes) and consistent character details. The goal was to create a specialized model for high-end cinematic portraiture.

THE SOLUTION

I utilized Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA) to fine-tune Stable Diffusion 1.5 on a curated dataset of 500+ cinematic movie stills. The training process involved tagging images with specific lighting directions and camera angles. The result is a lightweight model capable of generating photorealistic portraits with specific lighting control via text prompts.
